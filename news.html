Imagine a future where the boundaries between the physical and virtual worlds dissolve, immersing us in a realm of Surreality where reality and fantasy seamlessly intertwine. We invite visionary artists, designers, and technologists to join us in shaping this realm by submitting artwork to the groundbreaking exhibition, SURREALITY.

By integrating cutting-edge AI-generated content with AR technology, we will co-create transformative, immersive experiences across thousands of square meters of the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou) campus, redefining artistic expression and pushing the boundaries of creativity.
